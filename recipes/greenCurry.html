Green Curry

Return Home

Description

Thai green curry is a delicious curry meal from Thailand. It is made with coconut milk and kaffir lime leaves. At times this succulent curry meal can be knock-your-socks-off spicy. It also can be adapted for vegan diets.

Ingredients

  • Thai Basil
  • Bamboo Shoots
  • Red Bell Pepper
  • Green Curry Paste
  • Kaffir Lime Leaves
  • Full Fat Coconut Milk
  • Fish Sauce
  • Chicken Or Other Protein

Steps

  1. Make green curry paste in mortar and pestle.
  2. Make paste as fine as possible.
  3. Cook coconut milk until very thick. Reduce.
  4. Add the curry paste and saute for several minutes.
  5. Add chicken.
  6. Add coconut milk.
  7. Add lime leaves
  8. Add fish sauce
  9. Simmer until chicken is tender.
  10. Add bamboo shoots and red bell pepper
  11. When green curry is finished, garnish with thai basil and serve with jasmine rice.
