Pad Thai Chicken

Description

A popular, incredibly famous dish from Thailand. Dig your teeth into these soft succulent noodles bursting with flavor.

Can be made with shrimp, beef, or virtually any other protein. This dish will wow your friends and family.

Ingredients

  • Vegetable Oil
  • 1/2 Onion
  • 2 Garlic Cloves
  • Chicken Breast
  • 2 Eggs
  • Beansprouts
  • Peanuts finely chopped

Steps

  1. Place noodles in bowl, soak for several minutes in boiling water.
  2. Mix Sauce in small bowl.
  3. Heat oil in large pan.
  4. Cook garlic and onion
  5. Add chicken.
  6. Add egg.
  7. Add bean sprouts
  8. Add peanuts
