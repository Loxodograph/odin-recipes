Thai Basil Chicken

Return Home

Description

This meal can be made very quickly. It is extremely fresh and flavorful. Spices, bell peppers, and fresh thai basil are the cornerstones of this dish.

This recipe is made with chicken but you can substitute any protein to adapt this recipe to your diet.

Ingredients

  • Thai Basil
  • Canola Oil
  • Garlic Cloves
  • Red Peppers
  • Thai Chili
  • Soy Sauce
  • Fish Sauce
  • Chicken Or Other Protein

Steps

  1. Cook Chicken
  2. Add Veggies
  3. Create basil sauce
  4. Add the basil sauce
  5. Add basil
  6. Add coconut milk.
